Terms and Conditions

Do's:

  • Ensure your credentials are genuine. If wrong credentials are found, your entry will lead to immediate rejection.
  • Logo must be related to GTU Innovation Council. Use of word GTU Innovation Council is not necessary.
  • Logo submitted must be original and should not be copied from anywhere else. GIC is responsible for any copyright violation.
  • Meaning of Logo must be easy to understand.
  • Submissions can be in any of the following formate:
    (i)Sketch - 6*6 inches.
    (ii)Digital images - size may vary.
  • By submitting logo, you agree that GTU Innovation Council reserves the right to use your logo on social media pages.
  • Submission must be in JPG/PNG/JPEG/SVG format with size limited to 4MB.

Dont's:

  • Any sort of watermark or imprint will lead to immediate rejection.
  • Logo must not have any sort of objectional or inappropriate content.
  • Group entries will not be entertained.
